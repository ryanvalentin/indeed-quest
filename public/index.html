IndeedQuest

Playable 16-bit RPG "day in the office" game, where you (Jobby) work from 9 to 5, helping Indeedians help people get jobs!

But if you fail, DIRE CONSEQUENCES await you!

Complete the quests of your coworkers or just take it easy today and enjoy their company.

Controllers:

  • Left/right/up/down arrows to move Jobby
  • Use your mouse to click on the dialog icons / interactive items
